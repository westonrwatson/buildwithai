Before we build

Install guide.

Step 01
Connect to WiFi first.
Join the workshop network before creating any accounts or downloading anything.
Network: Liahona
Password: alma3738
Step 02
Create your accounts.
Open each link and sign up. Use the same email for all three — it'll make things simpler later.
claude.ai github.com/login vercel.com/signup
Step 03
Download Cursor.
Go to cursor.com/download and install for your OS. Takes about 2 minutes. Once installed, sign in with your GitHub account when prompted.
cursor.com/download
Step 04
Create your first project.
Open Cursor → Open Project and create a new folder for your work (e.g. my-wordle). Open the chat panel on the right — that's where you'll talk to the AI.
Step 05
You're ready.
All four tools are free. No credit card required anywhere. If anything isn't working, raise your hand and we'll sort it out. Then head to Lab 01.
